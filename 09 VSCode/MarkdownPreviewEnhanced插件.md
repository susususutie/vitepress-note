## 流程图 flow

```flow
st=>start: 起始框
e=>end: 结束框
op1=>operation: 处理框
sub1=>subroutine: 子进程
cond=>condition: 判断框
io=>inputoutput: 输入、输出框
st->op1->cond
cond(yes)->io->e
cond(no)->sub1(right)->op1
```
